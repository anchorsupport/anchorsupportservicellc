Eligibility Snapshot

Eligibility Snapshot

Tenancy Support Services are Medicaid-funded services for eligible Montana Medicaid members. Medicaid enrollment must be confirmed before a TSS referral can be processed. Eligibility is determined through the referral and assessment process.

Who may qualify

  • Adults 18 years of age or older
  • Enrolled in Montana Medicaid
  • Have a qualifying substance use disorder and/or serious mental illness, or symptoms that may require further assessment
  • Are experiencing homelessness, have a history of homelessness, or are at risk of housing instability
  • Need housing-related support connected to tenancy stability

Careful note

This information is a general overview only. Final eligibility is determined through the appropriate referral, assessment, and service planning process. Anchor Support Service LLC does not guarantee eligibility, housing placement, rental assistance, or service approval.
